Who I am

Hi, I am Anjali. By day I am a Software Engineer II at Movius, working on backend systems, real-time communication infrastructure, and LLM integrations. I like understanding how a product fits together end to end, not only the slice I happen to own.

By habit, I think out loud. Writing something down, in public, is how I work out what I actually believe about it. None of the pieces below are finished arguments. They are running notes, a map I am still tracing, and each one is a continuation of the last.

A little more

On the engineering side, most of my work lives in backend systems, real-time voice infrastructure, and putting language models to actual use inside a product. Before Movius I was a founding engineer at an early-stage startup and worked on data consistency tooling at Amazon Go, so I have seen both the zero-to-one scramble and the scale-everything end of the spectrum.

Away from the screen, I travel when I can (Puerto Rico was the most recent), cook vegetarian, and keep a running list of things I do not understand yet. The writing here is part of how that list gets shorter.
